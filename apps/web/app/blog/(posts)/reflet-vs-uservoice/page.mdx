### Where Reflet Excels

**Accessibility**: Start collecting feedback in minutes with a free tier. No sales calls, no demos, no procurement process.

**Modern Architecture**: Built on Convex for real-time synchronization, React 19, and Next.js — Reflet feels fast and responsive compared to UserVoice's aging interface.

**Open Source Freedom**: Inspect the code, self-host, contribute improvements. UserVoice is a complete black box.

**Pricing for Humans**: At $15-49/mo, Reflet is accessible to solo founders and startups. UserVoice's $799/mo minimum puts it out of reach for most teams.

### Where UserVoice Excels

**Enterprise Features**: Revenue-based prioritization, advanced segmentation, and Salesforce integration make UserVoice powerful for large B2B companies.

**NPS Integration**: Built-in NPS surveys help tie satisfaction scores to feedback and feature requests.

**Established Trust**: Fortune 500 companies use UserVoice, providing enterprise-grade compliance and support.

## When to Choose Reflet

- **You're a startup or growing team**: You need feedback tools without enterprise budgets
- **Speed matters**: You want to start collecting feedback today, not after a sales cycle
- **Open source is valued**: Code transparency and self-hosting are important
- **Modern UX matters**: You want a fast, responsive, real-time experience
- **Budget is a factor**: $799/mo is not justifiable for your current stage

## When to Choose UserVoice

- **You're a Fortune 500 company**: You need enterprise compliance and support
- **Revenue-weighted feedback is critical**: You need to prioritize by customer value
- **You have budget**: $799+/mo is within your feedback tooling budget
- **NPS integration is essential**: Built-in satisfaction surveys are a must

## The Verdict

UserVoice defined the feedback category but hasn't kept pace with modern expectations. Its enterprise pricing and sales-driven approach make it inaccessible to most teams.

**Choose Reflet** for a modern, open-source, affordable alternative that provides the core feedback features most teams need.

**Choose UserVoice** only if you're an enterprise with specific needs for revenue-based prioritization and a budget to match.
