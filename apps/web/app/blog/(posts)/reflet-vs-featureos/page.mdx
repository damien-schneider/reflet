### Where Reflet Excels

**Open Source Freedom**: Inspect the code, self-host, and customize. FeatureOS is closed-source and cloud-only.

**Real-Time Architecture**: Convex-powered sub-50ms synchronization creates a responsive, collaborative experience.

**AI Triage**: Automatic feedback categorization and duplicate detection, built on modern AI models.

**Pricing Stability**: Reflet's pricing is transparent and stable. FeatureOS has had frequent pricing changes, making budgeting unpredictable.

**Free Tier**: Start at no cost. FeatureOS starts at $29/mo.

### Where FeatureOS Excels

**Knowledge Base**: FeatureOS includes a built-in knowledge base / help center — useful if you don't have one already.

**Flexibility**: FeatureOS offers more customization options for feedback workflows and board layouts.

## When to Choose Reflet

- **Open source matters**: Code transparency and self-hosting are important
- **Real-time collaboration**: Instant updates create a better team experience
- **Predictable pricing**: You want stable, transparent pricing
- **AI features**: Automated triage helps manage feedback volume
- **Free to start**: Validate your workflow before paying

## When to Choose FeatureOS

- **Knowledge base needed**: You want a built-in help center alongside feedback
- **Workflow flexibility**: Advanced customization of feedback workflows is essential
- **Established tool**: You prefer a tool with a longer track record

## The Verdict

FeatureOS is a capable feedback platform, but its lack of open source, no free tier, and unpredictable pricing changes are drawbacks. Reflet offers more features per dollar with the transparency of open source.

**Choose Reflet** for a modern, open-source platform with AI features and transparent pricing.

**Choose FeatureOS** if you specifically need a built-in knowledge base alongside your feedback tools.
