### Where Reflet Excels

**Real-Time Collaboration**: Built on Convex, Reflet provides instant synchronization across all connected users. Changes appear immediately without page refreshes, creating a more responsive experience for teams managing feedback together.

**Open Source Flexibility**: Reflet's open-source nature means you can inspect the code, contribute improvements, and customize the platform to your exact needs. This transparency builds trust and enables deeper integrations.

**Self-Hosting Option**: For teams with strict data residency requirements or those who prefer complete control, Reflet can be deployed on your own infrastructure.

### Where Canny Excels

**AI Autopilot**: Canny offers AI-powered features that automatically categorize feedback, detect duplicates, and suggest related requests. This can save significant time for teams processing high volumes of feedback.

**Mature Ecosystem**: As an established player, Canny has a broader range of native integrations and a more extensive documentation library.

**Enterprise Features**: Canny's higher-tier plans include advanced features like SSO, dedicated support, and custom contracts.

## When to Choose Reflet

Reflet is the better choice when:

- **Budget matters**: You need powerful feedback tools without the premium price tag
- **Data ownership is important**: You want the option to self-host and control your data
- **Transparency is valued**: You prefer open-source software you can inspect and customize
- **Real-time collaboration is key**: Your team works closely together on feedback management
- **You're a startup or indie developer**: You need a cost-effective solution that scales with you

## When to Choose Canny

Canny might be the better choice when:

- **You need AI automation**: AI-powered categorization and duplicate detection are essential
- **Enterprise compliance is required**: You need SOC 2, HIPAA, or other compliance certifications
- **Budget is not a constraint**: You can afford premium pricing for a mature platform
- **You need specific integrations**: Canny has a native integration you require

## Migration Guide: Moving from Canny to Reflet

Thinking about switching? Here's how to migrate your feedback data:

### Step 1: Export Your Data from Canny

1. Go to your Canny admin dashboard
2. Navigate to Settings > Export
3. Download your posts, votes, and comments as CSV

### Step 2: Prepare Your Reflet Account

1. Sign up for Reflet at [reflet.app](/dashboard)
2. Create your organization and feedback boards
3. Invite your team members

### Step 3: Import Your Feedback

1. Map your Canny categories to Reflet boards
2. Use Reflet's import tools or API to migrate feedback items
3. Verify that votes and comments are preserved

### Step 4: Update Your Embeds and Links

1. Replace Canny widgets with Reflet embeds
2. Update any documentation or links pointing to your old feedback portal
3. Set up redirects if using custom domains

## The Verdict

Both Reflet and Canny are capable product feedback platforms, but they serve different needs.

**Choose Reflet** if you value open-source software, need budget-friendly pricing, want self-hosting options, or require real-time collaboration features.

**Choose Canny** if you need AI automation, require enterprise compliance features, or have specific integration requirements that only Canny supports.

For most startups, indie developers, and growing teams, Reflet provides the best balance of features, flexibility, and value.
