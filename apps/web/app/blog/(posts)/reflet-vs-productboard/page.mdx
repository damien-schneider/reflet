### Where Reflet Excels

**Simplicity and Focus**: Reflet does one thing exceptionally well — collecting user feedback, managing feature requests, and sharing roadmaps. There's no learning curve for advanced product management frameworks you may not need.

**Cost Efficiency**: At $15-49/mo compared to Productboard's $75/maker/month, Reflet is dramatically more affordable. A 5-person team would pay $49/mo with Reflet vs $375/mo with Productboard.

**Open Source Transparency**: With Reflet, you can inspect every line of code, self-host for complete data ownership, and customize the platform to your needs.

**Public Feature Voting**: Reflet's voting system is a first-class feature, making it easy for users to express which features matter most. Productboard's voting is more limited in comparison.

### Where Productboard Excels

**Prioritization Frameworks**: Productboard offers sophisticated prioritization with weighted scoring, RICE framework, and value-vs-effort matrices — ideal for large product organizations.

**Customer Intelligence**: Productboard's Insights portal aggregates feedback from sales calls, support tickets, and research into a unified view tied to customer segments and revenue data.

**Enterprise Scale**: Built for large organizations with multiple product lines, Productboard handles complex hierarchies, permissions, and cross-team coordination.

## When to Choose Reflet

Reflet is the better choice when:

- **You need feedback-focused tools**: Your primary need is collecting user feedback and sharing roadmaps, not full product management
- **Budget matters**: You want powerful tools without enterprise-level pricing
- **Open source is important**: You value code transparency, data ownership, and self-hosting
- **You're a startup or indie dev**: You need to move fast with a lightweight, focused tool
- **Real-time collaboration is key**: Your team works closely and needs instant updates

## When to Choose Productboard

Productboard might be better when:

- **You need advanced prioritization**: RICE scoring, value-effort matrices, and weighted frameworks are essential
- **Customer intelligence is critical**: You need to tie feedback to customer segments and revenue
- **You're a large product org**: Multiple product lines with complex team structures
- **Budget is flexible**: You can invest $75+/maker/month for a comprehensive suite

## The Verdict

Productboard is a powerful, enterprise-grade product management platform — but that power comes with complexity and cost that many teams don't need.

**Choose Reflet** if you want a focused, affordable, open-source feedback platform that does the core job brilliantly.

**Choose Productboard** if you need enterprise-scale product management with advanced prioritization and customer intelligence.

For most startups, indie developers, and growing teams, Reflet delivers the feedback and roadmap features they need at a fraction of the cost.
