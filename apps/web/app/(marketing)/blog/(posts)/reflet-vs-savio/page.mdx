### Where Reflet Excels

**Public Engagement**: Reflet's public feedback boards and voting system create genuine user engagement. Savio focuses more on internal team workflows.

**Pricing**: At $15-49/mo vs Savio's $79-299/mo, Reflet is significantly more affordable.

**Open Source**: Full code transparency, self-hosting, and community contributions.

**AI Triage**: Automated categorization and duplicate detection.

**Real-Time**: Sub-50ms synchronization for instant collaboration.

### Where Savio Excels

**Feedback Centralization**: Savio excels at pulling feedback from Slack, HubSpot, Intercom, Zendesk, and Salesforce into one place.

**Revenue Attribution**: Tie feature requests to customer MRR, making it easier to prioritize high-value feedback.

**B2B Workflows**: Purpose-built for B2B SaaS teams that manage feedback across sales, support, and customer success.

## When to Choose Reflet

- **Public-facing feedback**: You want a user-facing feedback board with voting
- **Budget matters**: Savio's pricing is 3-6x higher than Reflet's
- **Open source is important**: Code transparency and self-hosting
- **You're not B2B enterprise**: Your feedback workflow doesn't need CRM integration
- **Developer workflow**: GitHub integration is your priority

## When to Choose Savio

- **Multi-source centralization**: You need to pull feedback from 5+ tools
- **Revenue-based prioritization**: Tying feedback to customer MRR is critical
- **B2B SaaS**: You have sales, support, and CS teams all collecting feedback
- **Salesforce integration**: CRM sync is a must-have

## The Verdict

Savio and Reflet serve different primary use cases. Savio is a B2B feedback centralization tool. Reflet is a public-facing feedback platform.

**Choose Reflet** if you want user-facing feedback boards, voting, roadmaps, and changelogs — at an affordable price with open-source benefits.

**Choose Savio** if you specifically need to centralize internal feedback from CRM and support tools with revenue attribution.
